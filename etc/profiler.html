1. Open your browser (Chrome works best but Safari and Firefox work too, no IE please)
  2. Drag this link PROFILER into your browser's bookmark bar (no need to click it first, just drag it straight from this page)
  3. Navigate to http://www.chegg.com/etextbooks/beta and sign in with user ereader1@chegg.com and password testing
  4. Click the big orange READ NOW button
  5. A book will come up, now click the 'profiler' bookmark we previously dragged into the browser's bookmark bar, the book should re-load
  6. Click the 'profiler' bookmark again and you should see a red box show up in the top-right corner of the book, you're doing great!
  7. Lets play with the book - scroll down a few pages and wait for each page to load, 5-10 pages is great - Jump around, do some reading, its fun
  8. Click the Magnifying Glass icon and then type the word study into the search box and click any of the search results
  9. Remember the red box - click it now, a screen will come up with lots of text - copy that text and send it back to ohad@chegg.com
